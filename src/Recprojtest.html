FAQ

Prototyping! Prototyping is my favorite step in building apps and/or pages. I have really enjoyed creating mockups on paper and in XD and then testing them out on anyone I can find.

I use it all the time, I find this field applies to most things I do. When starting up a process or project, I try to interview the customer and use the learned methods of User Research to get to the heart of the need and to find an efficient solution.

I do, I see it as relevent as it always has been. The mechanics of web pages or aps may be able to be completed quickly and efficiently with AI but tackling the right problems and getting to the heart of what can be done to improve things will still need to investigated by humans.

Someone that I have really enjoyed reading and studying is Don Norman. He was one of the founders of the Apple User Experience team. He is also a professor and author. I enjoyed reading his book "The Design of Everyday Things"
